Security & Compliance
How We Keep Your Users — and You — Protected

HIPAA compliance isn't something added at the end — it's built into the foundation from day one. Here's exactly what that means for your app and for your legal protection as the product owner.

🔒
Encryption Everywhere
All data in your database is encrypted at rest. All data moving between your users and the app travels over HTTPS. Nothing is ever exposed in plain text — anywhere in the system.
📋
Full Audit Logs
Every action taken in your app — login, assessment started, result viewed — is recorded automatically. HIPAA requires this, and it protects you legally if questions ever arise.
🏥
Business Associate Agreement (BAA)
AWS will sign a BAA with you — a legal requirement for apps touching Protected Health Information. We set this up before any code is written so you're covered from the very start.
👤
Access Controls & MFA
Every user only sees their own data. You have a separate admin view. Multi-factor authentication required for all accounts. Nobody gets in who shouldn't be there.
🔐
Enterprise-Grade Authentication
We use Auth0 or AWS Cognito — the same identity platforms used by Fortune 500 companies. Handles session management, password resets, and brute-force protection automatically.
⚖️
Attorney Review Before Launch
We build to technical HIPAA standards. Before launch, we recommend a HIPAA attorney review your privacy policy and terms of service. That's your personal legal protection as the owner.
Technology
What We're Building It With — and Why

Every technology choice here was made deliberately for security, reliability, and long-term maintainability. Nothing exotic, nothing that locks you in, nothing you can't hand to another developer if you ever need to.

What Your Users See
React.js + Tailwind CSS
Industry standard for web apps. Handles your multi-step assessment flow cleanly, looks polished on every device, and is easy to maintain and expand as your product grows.
Your Decision Engine
Python / FastAPI
The dominant language in healthcare tech. FastAPI has strong data validation built in — critical when inputs going into your decision engine need to be completely trustworthy.
Your Data
PostgreSQL
Battle-tested and reliable. Handles all your relational data — users, assessments, results, audit logs — all linked together correctly. Supports encryption at rest natively.
Where It Lives
Amazon Web Services (AWS)
One of the only cloud providers that offers HIPAA-eligible infrastructure and will sign a BAA. Also scales easily as your user base grows without rebuilding anything.
Login Security
Auth0 or AWS Cognito
Enterprise-grade identity management. Handles MFA, secure sessions, password resets, and role-based access. Far more secure than building a login system from scratch.
Payments (If Needed)
Stripe
If your app runs on subscriptions, Stripe handles all billing, invoicing, and recurring payments cleanly. Trusted by millions of businesses worldwide.
